15 ChatGPT Prompts for Etsy Sellers

Write better product listings, rank higher in Etsy search, and handle customer messages 10x faster — with AI prompts that actually understand how Etsy works.

Most Etsy sellers use ChatGPT the wrong way: they type "write me a product description" and get generic output that sounds like every other listing. The difference is in the prompt.

These 15 prompts are built around how Etsy's algorithm actually works — what factors influence search ranking, what buyers respond to emotionally, and what separates a $200/month shop from a $5,000/month shop.

Copy and paste these into ChatGPT (free or Plus), Claude, or Gemini. Fill in the brackets with your specific details.

Etsy SEO: Titles & Tags

1. The SEO Title Generator

Etsy titles are one of the most important ranking factors. This prompt writes titles that balance keyword density with readability.

Act as an Etsy SEO expert. I sell [product] in my shop [shop name/description]. My target buyer is [describe: e.g., new moms looking for personalized baby gifts]. Write 5 Etsy title variations (max 140 characters each) that: 1) Start with the primary keyword buyers would actually search, 2) Include 3-4 secondary keywords naturally, 3) Sound appealing to humans, not just algorithms. Also identify the 13 best tags I should use.

2. The Long-Tail Keyword Finder

Competing on broad keywords is hard. Long-tail keywords have less competition and higher conversion intent.

I sell [product type] on Etsy. Help me find 20 specific long-tail keyword phrases that buyers actually search when they're ready to buy (high purchase intent, not just browsing). Focus on: specific occasions (weddings, birthdays, graduations), specific materials or styles, specific uses or recipients. For each keyword, tell me why it has buyer intent.

3. The Competitor Analysis Prompt

I'm trying to rank for the keyword "[target keyword]" on Etsy. Here are the titles of 5 top-ranking listings for this keyword: [paste 5 titles]. Analyze what keywords they're using, what's missing that I could capture, and write me 3 title options that could compete with or differentiate from these listings.

Product Descriptions That Convert

4. The Emotion-First Description

The best Etsy descriptions sell the feeling, not just the product.

Write a product description for my Etsy listing: [product name and details]. My target buyer is [describe ideal customer]. The emotional reason they're buying this is [e.g., they want to feel like a good parent / they want to impress their boss / they want to treat themselves]. Structure the description as: 1) Emotional hook (2 sentences about the feeling/moment this product creates), 2) Product details (what it is, dimensions, materials), 3) Who it's perfect for, 4) How to order/customize, 5) Shipping and care info. Keep it under 400 words.

5. The Gift Listing Optimizer

Gift searches are huge on Etsy. This prompt rewrites your listing to capture gift-searchers.

Rewrite my product listing for people searching for gifts. Current listing: [paste your listing]. Transform it to: 1) Lead with gift-giving scenarios (birthdays, anniversaries, Christmas, Mother's Day), 2) Emphasize that it arrives gift-ready or can be personalized, 3) Include gift-related search phrases naturally, 4) Add a note about the emotional value as a gift. Keep the product details accurate.

6. The Personalization Upsell Script

I sell [product] and offer personalization/customization for an extra $[amount]. Many buyers don't realize they can customize it. Write a section I can add to my product description that: naturally mentions the personalization option, gives examples of what can be customized, explains how to submit their customization details, and creates slight urgency around ordering early enough for the customization to be ready by [holiday/occasion].

Customer Communication

7. The Review Request Message

Reviews drive Etsy sales. Most sellers don't ask — and miss out on dozens of reviews.

Write a post-purchase message I can send to buyers 3-5 days after delivery. It should: feel personal and warm (not automated), ask for a review without being pushy or violating Etsy's policies, mention that I'm a small shop where reviews make a real difference, offer to help if anything was wrong with the order, and subtly mention my other products. Keep it under 150 words.

8. The Difficult Customer Response

Every Etsy seller eventually gets an unfair complaint. This prompt helps you respond professionally.

I received this message from a buyer: "[paste message]". The situation is: [explain what actually happened]. I need to respond in a way that: 1) Acknowledges their frustration without admitting fault for something that wasn't my fault, 2) Offers a fair resolution, 3) Protects me from an unfair review, 4) Stays professional even if they were rude. Write 2 response options — one more accommodating, one firmer.

9. The Custom Order Clarification Script

A buyer sent me this custom order request: "[paste message]". There are some details missing that I need to complete the order: [list what's missing]. Write a friendly, professional response that: thanks them for their interest, explains what specific information I need, asks all my questions in one message (not one at a time), and gives them an estimated timeline once I have the details.

Shop Strategy & Growth

10. The Pricing Analysis

Help me price my [product] correctly. My costs: Materials = $[X], Time = [Y hours] at $[Z/hour], Etsy fees = 15-20% of sale price, Shipping supplies = $[A]. I want a profit margin of at least [B]%. I've found similar products on Etsy ranging from $[low] to $[high]. Calculate my break-even price, recommend a retail price that's competitive but profitable, and suggest a sale price for running promotions.

11. The New Product Validation Prompt

Before spending hours making a new product, validate demand first.

I'm considering adding [new product idea] to my Etsy shop that currently sells [existing products]. Evaluate this idea by: 1) Estimating the search demand (high/medium/low) for this product type on Etsy, 2) Identifying the top 5 direct competitors I'd be fighting for visibility against, 3) Analyzing whether this product fits my existing audience or targets a new one, 4) Recommending whether I should proceed, test with 1 listing, or skip it. Be direct about the risks.

12. The Seasonal Campaign Planner

Create a 60-day promotional plan for my Etsy shop leading up to [holiday/event]. My shop sells [product types]. The plan should include: 1) Key dates to start promoting (not just the holiday itself), 2) What listing optimizations to make and when, 3) A social media content schedule (3 posts/week), 4) A sale or promotion strategy including timing and discount levels, 5) How to handle the post-holiday period to maintain momentum.

Shop Branding & Copy

13. The Shop Announcement Writer

Write an Etsy shop announcement for my shop that: introduces who I am in 2 sentences, explains what makes my products different (not generic "handmade with love" filler), mentions any current promotions or new items, and ends with a clear next step for visitors. My shop: [describe your shop, products, and what makes you different]. Keep it under 250 words and make it feel like a real person, not a press release.

14. The About Page Rewrite

Rewrite my Etsy About page to build buyer trust and emotional connection. Current version: [paste your about page]. The rewrite should: tell a 3-sentence origin story of how I started making these products, explain my process or craft (what buyers should appreciate about the skill involved), share a personal detail that makes me relatable, and explain my values as a seller (quality, customer service, sustainability, etc.). Make it feel authentic, not corporate.

15. The FAQ Section Builder

Write a comprehensive FAQ section for my Etsy shop that preemptively answers buyer concerns. My shop sells [products] and I commonly get questions about: [list common questions you receive]. Also include FAQ answers for: custom orders, shipping times, returns/exchanges policy, care instructions, and gifting options. Write in a warm, reassuring tone that builds trust rather than sounding defensive.
Time-saving tip: Create a "prompts" folder in your notes app with the ones you use most. Etsy shop management works best when you can quickly pull up the right prompt — don't rely on finding this article every time.
